Hello and welcome to the test!

Please read these instructions carefully before going forward.

This test is designed as part of a pilot study into the generation algorithm for signals used in alarm systems. By taking part, you will contribute to the study, and provide valuable data which is used to evaluate the effectiveness of the algorithm being developed.

How the test works:

You will be presented with sounds over either 10, 30 or 50 trials, twice.

The trial will consist of listening to some noise and adjusting a slider until you hear a tone.

Your aim is to adjust the volume of the tone until it is just audible over the noise.

You will perform the test in 2 rounds, and there will be a short pause between each round.

On the next page, there will be a small training example, and a chance for you to calibrate your audio levels and listening. You participation is appreciated. If you do not wish to take part, please simply close this browser window. You may exit the test at any time by closing the browser window.

This listening test is run as part of the PATERSON project at Universidade do Minho, Portugal.
